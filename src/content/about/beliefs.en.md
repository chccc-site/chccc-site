---
title: Our Beliefs
---

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the supreme authority for faith and life.

We believe in the triune God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, who died on the cross for our sins, rose from the dead on the third day, and ascended to sit at the right hand of God the Father.

We believe the Holy Spirit dwells in every believer, guiding us to live holy lives.

We believe the church is the body of Christ, where believers love and build up one another.

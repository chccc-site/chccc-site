---
title: Church History
---

Cherry Hill Chinese Christian Church was founded over twenty years ago by a group of devoted brothers and sisters in Cherry Hill, New Jersey. Starting from humble home gatherings, the church has grown steadily and now meets at its own building at 299 Browning Lane.

The church offers worship services in Mandarin, Cantonese, and English, serving the diverse Chinese community. Our vision is to be a loving, multilingual community that welcomes everyone.

---
title: Church History
---

CHCCC originated from the Chinese Mission of our "mother" church — Cherry Hill Baptist Church (CHBC). In 2000, several Chinese families attending CHBC started a family fellowship and Bible study group. With rapid growth of this Chinese fellowship group, in October 2002, CHBC launched the Chinese Mission with a separate Sunday worship service in Mandarin. In May 2004, the Chinese Mission launched out as a fledgling independent church: Cherry Hill Chinese Christian Church. The two congregations worked together in the children and youth ministries until April 2008 when CHBC closed and CHCCC assumed stewardship of the church facilities.

At the end of May 2008, Cherry Hill Chinese Christian Church took over all the building facilities from our mother church CHBC, beginning a brand new chapter in the development of our church ministry. It was the Holy Spirit's own leading that moved the brothers and sisters of our mother church to let us inherit this place, making us the only Chinese church in South Jersey to own its own church facilities. We firmly believe that God has given us this blessing — far exceeding all that we could ask or imagine — because He loves our Chinese compatriots and wants us to share in the Great Commission entrusted by the Lord Jesus Christ, so that His tent may be greatly expanded in the South Jersey area.
